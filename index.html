What I'm doing now

All-in on AI, at Forum3

In 2021 I co-founded Forum3 with Adam Brotman, Starbucks' former Chief Digital Officer. We helped build Starbucks Odyssey, raised $10M, and today Forum3 works as an embedded AI and agent strategy partner for consumer brands.

"Cut through the AI noise."

I'm also Managing Partner of Keen Capital, a fund of blockchain-native venture funds, and I host the AI First podcast.
